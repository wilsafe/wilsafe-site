Training of Trainers

TOT, or Training of Trainers, is the course that will help you build on your existing instructional skills, giving you multiple opportunities to evaluate and improve the way you design, conduct, and evaluate a training event. With a strong focus on presentation skills and training best practices, you will gain valuable knowledge in this practical and constructive learning environment.

Course Duration: Three (3) Days

Day 1

Module 1: Introduction to training

· Learning styles

· Adult learning principles

· Training design

Module 2: Identify training needs

· Analyzing training needs

· 5Ws of needs analysis

Module 3: Selecting training methods

· Training method

· Criteria in selecting training method

Day 2

Module 4: Create a lesson plan

· Purpose of a lesson plan

· Developing learning objectives

· Outlining training content

· Lesson Plan templates

Module 5: Facilitate great training

· Facilitations skills

· Training facilities

· Seating arrangements

· Effective training aids

Day 3

Module 6: Evaluate your training

· Why evaluate training

· Training evaluation

Assessment

· Multiple choice written exam

· Micro-teaching session

Course Certificate: A Certificate of Completion will be given to all those who successfully complete the 3-days training program, and pass the assessment.
